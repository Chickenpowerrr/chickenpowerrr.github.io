About

I am a PhD candidate in the Formal Methods and Tools (FMT) group at the University of Twente, supervised by Arnd Hartmanns. My research focuses on probabilistic model checking, with a particular interest in multi-objective model checking.

Before my PhD, I completed an MSc in Computer Science and a BSc in Technical Computer Science, both at the University of Twente. My MSc thesis focused on the state of multi-objective model checking algorithms and tools.
